DID YOU KNOW?
🔒

EVMs are standalone machines with no internet or Bluetooth connection.

🖨️

VVPAT was introduced in 2013 to let voters verify their vote visually.

⚡

EVMs run on 7.5V alkaline batteries and work without electricity.

🛡️

The EVM memory chip is one-time programmable and cannot be rewritten.

🗳️

A single EVM can record up to 2,000 votes with 64 candidate capacity.
